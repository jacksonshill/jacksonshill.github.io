Magnificant Wines

Jackson's Hill Reserve Semillon 2016

In the glass: Bright pale yellow.
On the nose: Delicate mix of yellow fruits, lime leaf and lemon curd.
On the palate: Light and dry yet quite intense and textural with understated citrus and light stonefruit flavours and mouth-watering texture. The mid-palate is quite deep with vibrant lemony acidity driving the long finish.

Almost water white with flashes of lemon, it has a nose displaying preserved lemon, talc and herbs. Lemon and lemongrass flavours rule the palate, which has zesty energy, while haunting chalky textural notes build complexity and give the citrus fruits a long platform.

Jackson's Hill The Under Block Semillon 2012

Despite a frustrating vintage, the grapes were pristine and the inclusion of fermentation solids resulted in a wine with lovely texture and concentration.

It’s pale lemon in the glass with preserved lemon, hints of herb and talc on the nose. The intention when making this wine was to impart texture and the result is a full and chewy palate, making it a classic food wine.

Jackson's Hill Chardonnay 2013

Embracing the finesse of cool climate Chardonnay, this Chardonnay includes fruit from Tumbarumba for this Wine Selectors exclusive.

Savoury aromas of white peach, lemon zest and citrus blossom with a sea spray-like minerality lead to a vibrant yet restrained palate. A umami-like layer adds depth while lees bring cashew richness and pristine acidity ensures cellaring potential.

Jackson's Hill Claret 2009

This Trophy and Gold medal-winning Cabernet Sauvignon and Cabernet Franc blend was named ‘Claret’ as a homage to the Australian Clarets that came before it - a term which will soon disappear from Australian red ‘Bordeaux’ blends.

In the glass: Brick red.
On the nose: Fragrant with a complexing smokey character.
On the palate: Quite silky and elegant in structure. The tannins are present, but not dominating, and three-year-old oak ties the Cabernet Sauvignon and Cabernet Franc together in a harmonious effort.

Jackson's Hill Sparking Pinot Noir Chardonnay 2006

Hunter Valley winery Jackson’s Hill has sourced cool climate fruit from Tasmania for this beautiful aged Sparkling.

Medium straw in the glass with a fine persistent bead, it displays attractive aromas of baked apple with touches of bottle aged honey and toast. The smooth, creamy and generous palate is developing well, showing long leesy richness underpinning generous stonefruit and preserved lemon. A beautiful aged style which is close to showing its best.
